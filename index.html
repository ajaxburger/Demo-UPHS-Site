About us

Pursuing academic and personal excellence.

At Upper Perkiomen High School, we focus on a modern approach to teaching, learning, and college and career readiness. Through collaboration andengagementin a personalized learning environment, we exceed these goals to build authentic connections and fully prepare students for a global society.

1 to 1 Technology

Students at the high school receive Windows computers to complete coursework and collaborate with other students from a modern approach. This provides students with what we refer to as digital equality. Regardless of socioeconomic status, Upper Perk strives to provide every student with access to online information and digital learning platforms.
